ROHAN DANIEL
Rohan Daniel Eason was an illustrator from Middlesborough and grew up in Lincolnshire. He always found drawing easier than other things at school. In his teenage years, he covered his bedroom walls with weird drawings of people, girls and monsters, fantastical nonsense, more a test of his ability than his knowledge of the world. Rohan also had played in a band which he was the lead guitar, but the dream of becoming a musician did not go in well.
